Research Interest

I am primarily interested in information retrieval and natural language processing, focusing on problems involving limited labeled data. This interest has led me to research domain adaptation, cross-lingual transfer learning, data augmentation, and diversification for active learning. I am currently exploring retrieval-based solutions to minimize fine-tuning frequency for deployed models.
